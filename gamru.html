G.A.M.R.U: Charity Organization Landing Page

An exploration of using visual design elements such as metaphor, metonymy, and synecdoche to portray a narrative, the potential landing page for G.A.M.R.U. both informs the viewer of the mission and history of the organization, as well as persuades them to donate.
